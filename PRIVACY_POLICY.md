# Privacy Policy for Tailors Suite

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Tailors Suite ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address and password for authentication
- **Client Data:** Names and contact information of your clients
- **Measurement Data:** Body measurements you record for your clients
- **Job Information:** Order details, due dates, and job descriptions
- **Payment Records:** Payment amounts and transaction history
- **Custom Content:** Logos and other customizations you upload

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App features you use and how you interact with the app
- **Error Logs:** Crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the Tailors Suite service
- Sync your data across your devices
- Improve and optimize app performance
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Respond to your support requests
- Send important service updates

## Data Storage and Security

- **Cloud Storage:** Your data is securely stored using Convex cloud services with encryption
- **Local Storage:** Data is cached locally on your device for offline access
- **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard security measures including:
  - Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  - Secure authentication
  - Regular security updates
  - Access controls

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your client data with anyone
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Access your data except for providing the service and troubleshooting

We may share data only in these limited circumstances:
- **With your consent:** When you explicitly authorize us to share information
- **Legal requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights
- **Service providers:** With trusted partners who help us operate the app (e.g., Convex for cloud storage)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Correction:** Update incorrect or incomplete data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Export:** Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out:** Discontinue use of the app at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at [YOUR_EMAIL_HERE]

## Data Retention

- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days
- Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes

## Children's Privacy

Tailors Suite is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

Tailors Suite uses the following third-party services:
- **Convex:** Cloud data storage and synchronization
- **Capacitor:** Mobile app framework

These services have their own privacy policies:
- Convex Privacy Policy: https://www.convex.dev/privacy
- Capacitor: https://capacitorjs.com (Ionic Framework)

## Offline Functionality

When you use Tailors Suite offline:
- Data is stored locally on your device
- Data syncs to the cloud when you reconnect
- You maintain full control over your data
